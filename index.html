dreamSACCO

DreamSacco is a modern and didgital solution that is designed to transform traditional saving groups(SACCOs) into a transparent, disciplined and efficicent experience for the youth in Uganda.

The Problem

Many young people struggle to maintain consistent saving habits due to manual and messy record keeping in notebooks, lack of transparency regarding funds and the complexity of management of loans and calculation of interests.

Key Features...

Private and public savings groups. Automated contribution tracking, a transparent real-time dashboard, an intelligent credit scoring engine. Contribution streak tracking. Multi-factor authentication on sensitive actions. Goal setting with progress tracking.

Authentication and Security: Multi-factor authentication and secure OTP verification.

Smart Loan System: Automated loan request flows and repayment schedules ranked by credit score.

Goal Tracking: Visual progress bars ad milestone notifications to keep users motivated.

Technologies

Flutter, Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Cloud Messaging.
